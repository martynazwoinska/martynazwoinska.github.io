# Game of Worms art catalogue

Last updated: 2026-07-14

This catalogue is the implementation contract for the 37 representative collection records in the Game of Worms. It separates verified collection evidence from the wider regional landscape and gives every location its own composition and accessory set.

## Rules

- Treat every row as a separate illustration, not as a shared ridge with interchangeable symbols.
- Keep the evidence and source URL already attached to each environment profile.
- F, M and B mean foreground, middle ground and background. Lock is the silhouette that must remain recognisable at thumbnail size.
- P and C are the primary- and companion-worm variants. They share a concept but require visibly different SVG geometry and remain independently movable.
- Never replace a drawing with an emoji, text label or generic costume carrying a different icon.
- Where exact collection substrate is absent, do not invent it or depict a precise specimen.
- Clothing and props are playful designs derived from geography, ecology, collection material or research activity. Do not present them as authentic local dress.
- Preserve N2’s Petri plate, fitted lab coat and cryo-vial jetpack.
- No sash or ordinary backpack is approved. N2’s scientifically motivated jetpack is the sole pack-like exception.
- Before revising a location, add a source-backed dossier under `docs/game-of-worms-location-research/`. Complete and review one location’s environment and three accessory pairs before starting another.
