## Purpose

Ho Chi Minh City · JU4356 environment and its three paired accessories.

## Evidence boundary

The carambola, its contact with the ground, the date, coordinates, urban-garden
landscape and strain history are exact record evidence.

A generic mature urban canopy, anonymous path edge and very narrow city-edge
glimpse are regional context. They must not be presented as a reconstruction
of the collection property.

Carambola morphology may follow authoritative botanical descriptions: a
drooping branched tree, pinnate leaves, small purple flowers and a strongly
ribbed fruit. The scene must not claim that a visible tree was the source of
the collected fruit.

## Later sequencing record

NCBI assembly GCA_977007565.1 is a later JU4356 genome assembly. Its linked
BioSample reports Hong Kong and 17 October 2024. Those fields describe later
sequencing-sample provenance and must not replace the documented wild
collection in Ho Chi Minh City on 30 August 2022.
