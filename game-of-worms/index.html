The Orchard · a first scenario

Begin a lineage

A young hermaphrodite in a fallen apple. Feed, learn which colonies to trust, read the changing air. When the apple turns, reproduce — or seal into dauer and ride to a fresh apple. Success is counted in descendants, not one life.

Every disposition has a cost. There is no strictly best choice — that is the point.

How to play

  • Move: arrow keys or WASD — or the round pad below on touch. Movement flows like a worm.
  • Feed: glide over a glowing colony. Rich colonies grow you; the harmful one costs you and teaches its smell.
  • Learn: once burned, a colony shows a dashed warning ring — steer around it.
  • Read the air: a sour, reddish haze warns that the apple may crash soon. It is usually — not always — right.
  • Dauer: if things turn while you are still young (L1–L2), you may enter L2d and then commit to dauer — sealed, tough, and able to travel.
  • Disperse: in dauer, reach a passing carrier (a snail) and nictate to ride to a fresh apple.
  • Reproduce: as an adult with reserves, self-fertilize, or outcross if a rare male appears.
